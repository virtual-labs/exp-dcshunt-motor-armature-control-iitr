The following procedure is to be followed for the simulation:

  1. Make the proper Connections as per the Instructions.


    • first of all connect the + (positive) terminal of MCB to the (-)negative terminal of Voltmeter(V) and to the motor's A2 and F2 Port respectively .

    • then connect - (negative) terminal of MCB to the L port of the Starter.

    • Connect The F port of the starter to the positive (+) terminal of the Ammeter(A).

    • Connect the A port of starter to the positive (+) terminal of the Armature Resistance .

    • Connect the positive(+) terminal of the voltmeter(V) to the negative(-) terminal of the Armature Resistance and to the motor's A1 Port Respectively

    • Connect Ammeter's negative(-) terminal to the positive(+) terminal of the Field Resistance.

    • Connect Negative (-)Terminal of the Field Resistance to the Port F1 of the Motor.

    • Connect Port A1 of the Motor to the Negative (-) terminal of the Armature Resistance.






2. Tap on the MCB to turn on the circuit.

3. Move slider second to get variations over the voltmeter readings.

4. Then tap on Button to values to table.

5. Repeat steps 3 & 4 to add more values in table

6.After getting 6 or more rows on table then click on to make a respective graph regarding the values in above table.

7. In order to print the page click on button .
